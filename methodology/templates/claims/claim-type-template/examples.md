## Anti-Patterns

### Common Failures

- The claim is too broad to support with one evidence thread.
- The claim is stated as an intention instead of an assertion the run is making.
- The observations restate conclusions instead of recording what was actually
  seen.
- The verdict overstates support relative to the observations.
- The evidence thread mixes this claim type with a nearby but different claim
  type.
