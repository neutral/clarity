# Step: capture-market-context

Purpose: Make market conditions and alternatives explicit for project docs.

Inputs:
- Target set (paths/headings/labels)
- Competitive research, benchmarks, or stakeholder input

Steps:
1) Summarize the market context or trends that shape the project docs.
2) List key competitors, substitutes, or alternatives.
3) Note differentiation, gaps, or positioning implications.
4) Link to sources or evidence when available.

Outputs:
- Market Context, Competitive Insight, Opportunity, and Benchmark statements.
