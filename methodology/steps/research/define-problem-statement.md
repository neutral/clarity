# Step: define-problem-statement

Purpose: Make the problem and affected audience explicit before solutioning.

Inputs:
- Target set (paths/headings/labels)
- Stakeholder input, research notes, or discovery summaries

Steps:
1) State the problem in one or two sentences without proposing a solution.
2) Identify the primary audience or customer segment affected.
3) Capture the impact and urgency (why it matters now).
4) Note any opportunity framing or success signal if known.

Outputs:
- Problem Statement, Audience, and Opportunity statements.
